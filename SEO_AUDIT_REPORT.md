## Detailed Ratings

### 1. Technical SEO: 8/10

-   **Strengths**: You have a solid technical foundation with a clean URL structure, a well-configured `robots.txt` file, and a dynamic sitemap. Your use of Next.js 14 and your focus on performance optimization are also commendable.
-   **Weaknesses**: Your sitemap contains links to non-existent pages, which can confuse search engines and waste crawl budget. You also need to address the issue of missing favicons and other image assets.
-   **Recommendations**:
    -   Remove the links to non-existent pages from your sitemap.
    -   Generate the required favicons and other image assets and add them to your `frontend/public` directory.

### 2. On-Page SEO: 7/10

-   **Strengths**: You have a comprehensive on-page SEO strategy with a clear focus on your target keywords. Your use of structured data and your attention to meta tags are also excellent.
-   **Weaknesses**: Your homepage is missing specific metadata, which is a missed opportunity to target your most important keywords. You also need to ensure that all of your pages have unique and compelling titles and descriptions.
-   **Recommendations**:
    -   Add specific metadata to your homepage to target your most important keywords.
    -   Review all of your pages to ensure that they have unique and compelling titles and descriptions.

### 3. Content SEO: 8/10

-   **Strengths**: You have a well-defined content strategy that targets a wide range of relevant keywords. Your focus on creating high-quality, long-form content is also a major strength.
-   **Weaknesses**: You need to create the city-specific landing pages that you've planned. You also need to ensure that your content is regularly updated and that you're consistently publishing new content to keep your site fresh and relevant.
-   **Recommendations**:
    -   Create the city-specific landing pages that you've planned.
    -   Develop a content calendar and a process for regularly updating your existing content and publishing new content.

### 4. Local SEO: 6/10

-   **Strengths**: You have a detailed local SEO strategy that targets 20+ major Indian cities. Your focus on creating city-specific landing pages and your use of local business schema are also excellent.
-   **Weaknesses**: You need to create the city-specific landing pages that you've planned. You also need to build local backlinks and citations to improve your local search rankings.
-   **Recommendations**:
    -   Create the city-specific landing pages that you've planned.
    -   Develop a strategy for building local backlinks and citations.

### 5. User Experience (UX): 7/10

-   **Strengths**: You have a clean and professional design, and your site is easy to navigate. Your focus on mobile-first design and your use of trust signals are also commendable.
-   **Weaknesses**: You need to address the issue of stale UI after logout. You also need to ensure that your site is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities.
-   **Recommendations**:
    -   Modify the `handleLogout` function to perform a hard reload after redirecting.
    -   Conduct a thorough accessibility audit of your site and address any issues that you find.
